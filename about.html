Who We Are

Licensed Electricians You Can Count On

Our licensed and experienced electricians bring years of hands-on expertise to every job. Whether it's a small residential repair or a complex commercial installation, we approach each project with the same commitment — professionalism, attention to detail, and a guarantee to get it right the first time.

Based in Kingston, Tennessee, Alpha9 Electric has served homeowners, businesses, and industrial facilities across the region for over 25 years. We show up on time, communicate clearly, and never cut corners — because your safety and satisfaction depend on it.

With Alpha9 Electric, you're not just hiring an electrician — you're partnering with a team that takes pride in every wire run, every panel installed, and every project completed. Workmanship built to last. Every time.
