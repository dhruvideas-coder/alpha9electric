Who We Are

Power Product Manufacturing & System Solution Provider

Founded with a clear mission — to deliver precision-engineered electrical products and solutions that power industries, protect homes, and accelerate businesses — Alpha9Electric has grown into a trusted name in the electrical manufacturing sector.

Based in Kingston, Tennessee, we combine over 30 years of hands-on experience with the latest engineering standards to design, manufacture, and install electrical systems that stand the test of time.

Our team of licensed engineers and electricians are committed to delivering quality, safety, and value on every project — no matter the scale or complexity.

Our Journey

Milestones That Define Our Legacy

1994

Company Founded

Alpha9Electric was established in Kingston, Tennessee, with a vision to provide precision-engineered electrical solutions to the region.

2002

Transformer Manufacturing Launched

Expanded into custom transformer manufacturing — distribution, power, and specialty transformers — becoming a regional leader in electrical products.

2010

Industrial Division Opened

Launched the industrial division offering complete PLC/VFD control panels, custom switchgear, and end-to-end industrial electrical solutions.

2018

500+ Projects Milestone

Celebrated delivering over 500 successful projects across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors — a testament to our team's dedication.

2024+

Continued Growth & Innovation

Expanding product lines, embracing smart electrical systems, and continuing to deliver excellence with every project we undertake.
