Who We Are

Licensed Electricians You Can Count On

Our licensed and experienced electricians bring years of hands-on expertise to every job. Our Journey

Milestones That Define Our Legacy

1994

Company Founded

Alpha9Electric was established in Kingston, Tennessee, with a commitment to delivering reliable, professional electrical services to homeowners and local businesses.

2002

Commercial Services Expanded

Expanded our service offering to commercial clients — retail spaces, office buildings, and multi-unit developments — growing into a trusted regional electrical contractor.

2010

Industrial Division Launched

Added a dedicated industrial services division — handling high-voltage installations, motor controls, PLC systems, and complex wiring for manufacturing facilities across Tennessee.

2018

500+ Projects Completed

Reached a major milestone — over 500 successfully completed projects across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors, and hundreds of satisfied clients.

2024+

Growing & Serving More Clients

Expanding our team, adding CCTV & security services, and continuing to set the standard for electrical craftsmanship across East Tennessee — one project at a time.
