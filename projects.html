Project FAQs

Everything you need to know before starting your electrical project with Alpha9 Electric.

What types of projects does Alpha9 Electric handle?

We handle a wide range of electrical projects — from residential rewiring and panel upgrades to full commercial fit-outs, industrial installations, EV charger setups, and CCTV/security system integrations.

How do I get a quote for my project?

Simply call us at (865) 290-0218 or fill out our contact form. We'll schedule a site visit or phone consultation to assess your needs and provide a detailed, no-obligation quote.

How long does a typical project take to complete?

Project timelines vary based on scope. Small residential jobs (outlets, fixtures, panel upgrades) are often completed in a single day. Larger commercial or industrial projects may span several days to weeks. We provide a clear timeline upfront.

Do you provide warranties on completed work?

Yes. All our work is backed by a craftsmanship warranty, and the materials and equipment we install come with manufacturer warranties. We stand behind every project we complete.

Are your electricians licensed and insured?

Absolutely. Alpha9 Electric is fully licensed, bonded, and insured in the state of Tennessee. Every project is completed by certified professionals who adhere to the latest NEC codes and local building regulations.
