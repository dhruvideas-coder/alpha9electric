General Questions

What services does Alpha9 Solutions offer?

We specialize in residential and commercial electrical services, including installation, maintenance, and repair. Additionally, we provide security camera solutions including installation, integration, and maintenance for CCTV systems.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, Alpha9 Solutions is fully licensed and insured to provide professional electrical and security camera services for both residential and commercial clients.

What areas do you serve?

We provide services across the East Tennessee region. Contact us to confirm if your specific location is within our immediate coverage area.

Electrical Services

Do you handle residential electrical repairs and installations?

Yes, we handle all types of residential electrical work, including whole-house wiring, lighting installations, panel upgrades, and detailed troubleshooting of electrical issues.

Can you assist with commercial electrical projects?

Absolutely! We specialize in commercial projects such as office wiring, breaker installations, industrial equipment connections, and energy-efficient lighting upgrades for businesses.

Do you offer electrical inspections?

Yes, we provide detailed electrical inspections to ensure your system meets all current safety standards, code requirements, and operates at peak efficiency.

Can you upgrade my electrical panel?

Yes, we offer heavy-up panel upgrade services to accommodate increased electrical loads, ensuring safety and compliance with modern standards for your home or business.

What should I do during a power outage or electrical emergency?

For your safety, switch off all major appliances to prevent surges and contact us immediately. We offer 24/7 troubleshooting and resolution for most electrical emergencies.

How often should I schedule electrical maintenance?

For residential properties, we recommend an annual inspection. For commercial establishments, regular maintenance every 6 months is advisable to prevent downtime.

Security Camera Services

What types of security cameras do you install?

We install various professional-grade cameras, including dome, bullet, PTZ (pan-tilt-zoom), and both robust wired and flexible wireless IP systems.

Can I monitor my security cameras remotely?

Yes, we specialize in remote-access setups that allow you to monitor high-definition footage via a secure smartphone app or web platform from anywhere in the world.

How long does it take to install a security camera system?

Install times vary. Small residential systems typically take a few hours, while larger commercial setups with complex integration may take a day or more.

Do you offer maintenance services for CCTV systems?

Yes, we provide routine maintenance including lens cleaning, connection integrity checks, storage optimization, and firmware updates.

How can I choose the best security camera system for my property?

Our experts perform a layout assessment of your property to recommend the best hardware based on your needs, budget, and desired features like night vision and motion detection.
