Service Area FAQs

Common questions about our coverage area and how we serve East Tennessee communities.

What cities and counties do you serve?

We serve 16+ cities across East Tennessee, including Knoxville, Kingston, Oak Ridge, Lenoir City, Maryville, Alcoa, Clinton, Farragut, Seymour, and surrounding communities in Roane, Knox, Anderson, Loudon, and Blount counties.

Do you travel to areas outside your listed coverage?

For larger commercial and industrial projects, we can extend our service range beyond the standard coverage area. Contact us to discuss your location and project scope — we'll do our best to accommodate you.

Is there a travel or service fee for outlying areas?

Service within our core coverage zone has no travel fee. For areas on the outer edges of our service map, a small travel charge may apply. We'll always be upfront about any fees before confirming your appointment.

How quickly can you respond to an emergency in my area?

We offer 24/7 emergency response across our entire service area. For most locations within East Tennessee, we aim to have a licensed electrician on-site within 1–2 hours of your call.

Do you offer the same services in all locations?

Yes. Whether you're in Knoxville or a smaller surrounding community, you receive the same full range of residential, commercial, and industrial electrical services — including panel upgrades, CCTV installations, and 24/7 emergency response.
